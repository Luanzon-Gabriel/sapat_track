ℹ️ How does this work?

Sapat Tracker uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — the most accurate method for estimating Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). Your BMR is then multiplied by your activity factor to get your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). Based on your goal, a calorie adjustment is applied to create your personalised daily target.

♂ Male BMR Formula
(10 × weight_kg) + (6.25 × height_cm) − (5 × age) + 5
♀ Female BMR Formula
(10 × weight_kg) + (6.25 × height_cm) − (5 × age) − 161
